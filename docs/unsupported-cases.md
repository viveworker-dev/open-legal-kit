# Unsupported Or High-Risk Cases

Do not treat this starter kit as enough if your app involves any of these cases.
Get qualified legal review before publishing.

- children or teen-directed products
- health, medical, therapy, wellness diagnosis, or clinical claims
- finance, credit, insurance, tax, investment, or trading
- employment, hiring, housing, education, or eligibility decisions
- biometric data, precise location, government IDs, or sensitive personal data
- sensitive file uploads unless storage, access control, deletion, and breach handling are reviewed
- crypto custody, asset management, lending, or investment claims
- marketplaces, escrow, dispute handling, or regulated payments
- user-generated public content at scale
- enterprise contracts, DPAs, BAAs, procurement terms, or security addenda
- apps offered at meaningful scale in the EU, UK, California, or other regulated markets
- apps that target users in jurisdictions you do not understand

This list is not complete. It is a set of starter warnings.
